ABOUT GFGC

Government First Grade College, Honnali is a Government of Karnataka run educational institution affiliated to DAVANGERE UNIVERSITY. This College aims at providing higher education to the students from rural and semi-urban areas of economically and socially backward classes. This college has educated many students, who have settled and attained good positions in the society. Government First Grade College, Honnali is a Government of Karnataka run educational institution affiliated to DAVANGERE UNIVERSITY. This College aims at providing higher education to the students from rural and semi-urban areas of economically and socially backward classes. This college has educated many students, who have settled and attained good positions in the society. A In August of 2007 the college came into existence with just 127 students enrolled for B.A., BBM, & B.COM, the very next year the no of students was doubled; at present the college has nearly a thousand students providing four degree courses, BA, B.sc, B.COM & B.B.M

INFRASTRUCTURE

Five acres of government land has been registered in the name of the Collegiate education department in which site a building of estimated cost of 121 lakhs is built, the eastern portion is built with the fund released by UGC 79 lakhs, in such building , 12 class rooms, a library an office room a staff room and principal chamber are available. Recently a grant of 1.85 crore has been sanctioned by the government of Karnataka. The construction work is yet to begin with the plan of 12 class rooms, 02 labs and one toilet facility in the first floor of the building
